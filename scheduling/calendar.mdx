The **Content Calendar** is your single view of everything that's posted, scheduled, or planned across your workspace. Open it from **Content Calendar** in the left sidebar.

<img
  src="/images/scheduling/calendar-week.png"
  alt="Carousify Content Calendar in Week View showing the scheduled post on the upcoming day"
  className="rounded-xl border"
/>

Switch between **Month View** and **Week View** from the dropdown at the top, and use the **All / Published / Scheduled** buttons to filter the grid.

The right-hand **Today's Schedule** panel lists every post queued for the current day with its time, type, and quick actions — **Publish Now** or **Edit**. Click any day on the grid to focus the panel on that day instead.
