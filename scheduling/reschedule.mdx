title: "Reschedule a post"
sidebarTitle: "Reschedule"
description: "Move a queued post to a different date or time without rewriting it."
---

Already scheduled a post but want to push it back, pull it forward, or move to a different day? Use **Re-Schedule** instead of canceling and starting over.

## Open the post

There are two ways in:

- From **Content Calendar**, click the scheduled post and pick **Edit**, or
- Open it from **Post Generator › Published** — scheduled posts live alongside published ones in this list

You'll land in the writer with a **Scheduled for [date, time]** strip at the bottom and a **Re-Schedule** button next to it.

## Click Re-Schedule

Clicking **Re-Schedule** opens the same publish dialog you used originally — pre-filled with the existing date, time, and any auto-plug settings.

<img
  src="/images/scheduling/reschedule-modal.png"
  alt="Re-Schedule dialog with the time field changed from 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM"
  className="rounded-xl border"
/>

Change the date or time (in this example, from **5:00 PM** to **8:00 PM**), then click **Re-Schedule** at the bottom of the dialog.

## Confirm

Carousify updates the queue and shows a **Post updated successfully** toast. The strip at the bottom of the editor now reflects the new time:

<img
  src="/images/scheduling/rescheduled-success.png"
  alt="Editor showing the post is now Scheduled for 8:00 PM after rescheduling"
  className="rounded-xl border"
/>

The change shows up immediately in **Content Calendar** too — the post moves to the new slot.
