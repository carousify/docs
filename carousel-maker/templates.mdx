Templates are full carousels — designed slides, real copy, complete layouts — that you open straight into the Carousel Maker. They are useful when you want a finished-looking deck without designing from scratch, or when you want a starting point for a recurring format you post often.

## Open the gallery

In the left sidebar, go to **Carousels › Templates**.

<img
  src="/images/carousel-maker/templates-gallery.png"
  alt="Carousify Template Gallery with carousel thumbnails laid out in a grid"
  className="rounded-xl border"
/>

The page is organised into five tabs:

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Templates" icon="grid">
    The Carousify-designed library. Your default starting point.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Drafts" icon="file-pen">
    Carousels you have started but not yet published. Same list as **Carousels › Drafts**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Saved" icon="bookmark">
    Decks you have kept around for re-use — your own templates.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Community Templates" icon="users">
    Decks shared by other Carousify users. Useful when the in-house library does not cover the style you need.
  </Card>
</Columns>

A **Trash** tab also shows up on the right — anything you delete from the other tabs lands there before being permanently removed.

## Use a template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick one from the gallery">
    Click any thumbnail. Carousify opens the deck inside the Carousel Maker with every slide editable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Replace the copy">
    Click any text element — heading, subheading, body, footer — and type your version. Layout, spacing, and effects stay intact.

    <img
      src="/images/carousel-maker/carousel-editor.png"
      alt="Template loaded in the Carousel Maker editor with a slide preview on the right"
      className="rounded-xl border"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply your Brand Kit">
    Open the **Brand Kit** tab and pick the kit for this workspace. Colors, fonts, and logos update across every slide at once — no slide-by-slide rework.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust slides as needed">
    Drag-and-drop in the slide tray to reorder. Duplicate or delete slides to match the length you want. **Reset Slide** undoes changes on the current slide if an edit goes wrong.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save, download, or publish">
    Drafts auto-save while you work. **Download** exports the deck as a PDF or PNG set, and **Publish** hands it off to the post composer with the carousel attached.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Most LinkedIn carousels do best in the 6–12 slide range. If a template runs longer, trim it before publishing — readers tend to drop off after slide 10.
</Tip>

## Build your own template library

- **Favorite from the gallery** — bookmark any deck from **Templates** or **Community Templates** and it lands in **Saved** for quick re-use.
- **Save your own as templates** — save any carousel you build (from scratch or modified from another template) as a template. It appears at the top of the **Templates** tab, above the Carousify-designed library.

<Note>
  Templates and Brand Kits are workspace-scoped. If you manage multiple clients in separate workspaces, save the templates inside each workspace where you will use them.
</Note>
