The **Post Generator** is where you write **text-first LinkedIn posts** in Carousify — single-image posts, plain text posts, and video posts. For multi-slide decks, head to the [Carousel Maker](/carousel-maker/overview).

Open it once and you get nine starting points: a blank canvas, four AI generators that turn audio, articles, YouTube videos, or PDFs into a draft, and four guided prompts for common post shapes. They all share the same writer at the end — but each one runs a different input flow and AI prompt before it gets there.

## Where to find it

In the left sidebar, open the **Post Generator** group.

| Page | What it is |
| --- | --- |
| **New Post** | The templates gallery — your nine starting points. |
| **Drafts** | Posts you've saved but not yet scheduled or published. |
| **Published** | Everything already live on LinkedIn, plus posts scheduled to go live. |
| **Trash** | Deleted drafts and posts. Restore from here or empty for good. |

The five tabs at the top of the gallery — **Post Templates / Drafts / Upcoming / Published / Trash** — surface the same lists with live counts.

</Note>
