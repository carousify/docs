The writer's top toolbar gives you the formatting controls LinkedIn supports. The right pane shows a live render of what the post will look like once it's live.

## Formatting toolbar

| Control | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **Bold** | Bold the selection |
| **Italic** | Italicize the selection |
| **Underline** | Underline the selection |
| **Strikethrough** | Strike through the selection |
| **Insert Emoji** | Open the emoji picker |
| **Add Image** | Attach a single image |
| **Add Video** | Attach a video |
| **Add Carousel** | Attach a deck built in the [Carousel Maker](/carousel-maker/overview) |

<Note>
  LinkedIn doesn't support real bold or italic in posts. Carousify converts your selections to **Unicode lookalike characters** that render the same way on every LinkedIn surface — but screen readers won't pronounce them as styled text. Use sparingly for emphasis, not for whole paragraphs.
</Note>

## Live preview

The right pane is a pixel-accurate render of the post. The **Devices** toggle switches between three layouts:

- **Mobile** — what most LinkedIn users will see
- **Tablet**
- **Desktop**

<Tip>
  Always check **Mobile** before publishing. Most LinkedIn traffic is on phones, and posts that look good on desktop sometimes break mid-hook on a small screen if line breaks land badly.
</Tip>
