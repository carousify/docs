Carousify has four AI generators that take an existing piece of content as input and produce a finished LinkedIn post draft. Same flow, same advanced options — only the input box changes.

| Source | Input | Best for |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Article URL** | Paste a link to a blog post or article | Repurposing long-form writing into a LinkedIn post |
| **YouTube video** | Paste a YouTube link | Drafting a post around a video — uses the video's description |
| **Audio** | Record live or upload a voice note | Turning a 60-second walk-and-talk into a written post |
| **PDF file** | Upload a PDF (max 20 MB) | Research papers, ebooks, reports, lead magnets |

<Note>
  Whichever source you start from, every generator drafts the post for you and drops it into the same editor — so you can run AI rewrites, fix grammar, or tighten copy before publishing. See [Write a new post](/post-generator/write-post#the-ai-assistant-panel) for the AI Assistant panel.
</Note>

## Open a generator

From **Post Generator › New Post** on the templates gallery, click the template that matches your input:

- **Generate from Content** — for article URLs
- **Generate from YouTube** — for video links
- **Generate from Audio** — for voice notes
- **Generate from PDF** — for documents

Every generator lands you on the same **Write Post** screen, with three tabs at the top of the input panel: **Input**, **Results**, and **Saved**.

## Provide the input

The input box is whatever the source needs.

### From an article URL

<img
  src="/images/post-generator/from-article.png"
  alt="Generate from Content tab with an Article URL input field"
  className="rounded-xl border"
/>

Paste the full URL of the blog post or article. Carousify reads the page and summarizes it into a post.

<Tip>
  Paywalled articles will not work — Carousify can only read what a public reader can. Use a public version or fall back to copying the text into [Write New Post](/post-generator/write-post) and rewriting from there.
</Tip>

### From a YouTube video

<img
  src="/images/post-generator/from-youtube.png"
  alt="Generate from YouTube tab with a YouTube video URL input field"
  className="rounded-xl border"
/>

Paste a YouTube link. Carousify reads the **video description** and drafts a post based on it.

### From audio

<img
  src="/images/post-generator/from-audio.png"
  alt="Generate from Audio tab with Record and Upload buttons"
  className="rounded-xl border"
/>

Two ways in:

- **Record** — click and speak. Carousify records straight from your browser.
- **Upload** — drag in an MP3, M4A, WAV, or similar audio file.

The audio is transcribed first, then the transcript is turned into a post.

<Tip>
  60 to 90 seconds of clear talking is the sweet spot. Longer than 3 minutes and the post tends to lose focus.
</Tip>

### From a PDF

<img
  src="/images/post-generator/from-pdf.png"
  alt="Generate from PDF tab with a Click to upload PDF dropzone"
  className="rounded-xl border"
/>

Click the dropzone and pick a PDF — research papers, ebooks, internal reports, lead magnets. **Maximum file size is 20 MB.**

## Show Advanced Options

Every generator has a **Show Advanced Options** toggle below the input. Flip it on to control how the post comes out.

<img
  src="/images/post-generator/from-article-advanced.png"
  alt="Advanced Options panel showing Post Format, Hashtags, Emojis, Tone of Voice, Approximate Words, and Audience fields"
  className="rounded-xl border"
/>

- **Select Post Format** — pick a preset post template
- **Generate Hashtags** — adds relevant hashtags at the end
- **Include Emojis** — sprinkles emojis through the post; off by default
- **Tone of Voice** — Polite, Witty, Enthusiastic, Friendly, Informational, or Funny
- **Approximate Words** — slider from 50 to 200; default is 70
- **Who is your audience?** — short description of who you're writing for. The more specific, the closer the post lands.

## Generate and refine

Below the input, the **Posts** counter sets how many variations Carousify should produce in one go — default is **3**. Tap `+` or `−` to change it, then hit **Generate Posts**.

Once a variation is loaded into the editor, treat it like any other draft:

- Use the **AI Assistant** panel below the editor — **Rephrase**, **Improve Writing**, **Fix Grammar**, **Make Shorter**, **Make Longer**, **Generate Hashtags**, or a free-form prompt
- Select a single line or paragraph and apply AI to just that part
- Run the **Analyzer** for a structured score and per-dimension suggestions

Full walkthrough on [Write a new post](/post-generator/write-post).

## Save Draft or Publish

When the post is ready:

- **Save Draft** lands the post in **Post Generator › Drafts**.
- **Publish** opens the **Publish Post** dialog — post now, [schedule](/scheduling/schedule-post) for later, or attach an [auto-plug](/scheduling/auto-plug) follow-up comment.
