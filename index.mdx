## How to get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [carousify.com](https://carousify.com/) and verify your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your workspace">
    During onboarding, set your workspace name, timezone, and other preferences.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect a LinkedIn account">
    Authorize Carousify using LinkedIn's official API. See [Connect your LinkedIn account](/getting-started/connect-linkedin).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your Brand Kit">
    Save your brand colors, fonts, and logos so every carousel matches. See [Brand Kit](/carousel-maker/brand-kit).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a plan">
    Pick a plan on the [pricing page](https://carousify.com/pricing/) to unlock the core features.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You can skip plan selection during onboarding, but creating carousels, generating posts, scheduling, and publishing all require an active subscription.
</Note>
