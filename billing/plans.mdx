Carousify offers four plans — **Hobby**, **Startup**, **Business**, and **Enterprise** — each available on **Monthly** or **Yearly** billing. This page covers what's in each plan and how to switch between them from the **Billing & Subscription** page.

<Note>
  Live pricing always lives at [carousify.com/pricing](https://carousify.com/pricing/). The numbers below reflect public pricing at the time of writing.
</Note>

## Where to find your plan

Open **Account menu › Billing** to see your current plan, renewal date, and plan history.

<img
  src="/images/billing/billing-overview.png"
  alt="Carousify Billing & Subscription page showing current plan, renewal date, and plan history"
  className="rounded-xl border"
/>

From this page you can:

- See your **current plan**, its renewal or validity date, and workspace usage against your limit
- Use **Change Plan** to switch tiers or billing cycle — see [Upgrade or downgrade](/billing/upgrade)
- Use **Cancel** to stop auto-renewal — disabled while a paid plan is active mid-cycle; see [Cancel your subscription](/billing/cancel)
- Browse **Plan History** for every plan you've held, with start and end dates and the license key it was activated with
- Click **Have a License Key?** to redeem a partner code
- Download an invoice next to any **Plan History** entry — Monthly and Yearly subscriptions only; Lifetime and license-key receipts come from the deal partner you bought through
